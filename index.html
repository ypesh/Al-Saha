Europe celebrates Imamat Day
To mark the 65th Imamat Day of our beloved Mawlana Hazar Imam, Jamats all over Europe celebrated this joyous occasion with a day of festivities on Sat 16 Jul. 2,700 murids from Jamats including Berlin, Hamburg, Essen, Munich, Frankfurt, Erfurt, Vienna, Warsaw, Graz and Amsterdam came together to commemorate the occasion. Jamati members were from Jamats including Syria, Afghanistan, Pakistan and Tajikistan who enjoyed music from their culture with traditional dance. The day was celebrated in decorated halls with dance, live music, a DJ and dinner and was made possible by the hard work of all volunteers.

Bowel Cancer Screening
The NHS conducts bowel cancer screening checks if you could have bowel cancer and is available to everyone aged 60-74. It's now easier than ever to get yourself checked through a home testing kit. Bowel cancer is the fourth most common type of cancer and screening can help prevent it or find it at an early stage when it is easier to treat. You should automatically be sent a home screening kit every two years. If you have ignored this kit and need another please contact your GP to arrange delivery. Click here for more info.

ITREB Thought of the Week
With its sharp pencil-like spires reaching to the sky and its magnificent domes and arches, the Hagia Sophia in Istanbul looks like something from a fairytale. It was a centre of Christian worship for almost 900 years, and today is a popular tourist destination. Under the rule of Justinian I in the 530s CE and his successors thereafter, it survived centuries of earthquakes, rebuilding and restoration. For Justinian I, the resurrection of Hagia Sophia was a major achievement. After 1453, the church was converted into a mosque under the Ottoman Muslim rule of Constantinople, afterwhich subsequent Ottoman sultans added to the building. In 1934, the secular government of Turkey turned the mosque into a museum, and the building continues to remind visitors of the importance of buildings as an exploration of religious and cultural expression.
